Definition

Restoration brings worn leather back to a wearable, dignified state — not new, but honest.

We work on calf, suede, exotic and aniline leathers. Handbags, jackets, shoes and sofas. Re-dyeing, structural repair, edge work, deep cleaning and conditioning — by hand, by a single dedicated artisan per piece.

For pieces too worn for a clean alone, but not beyond rebuilding.

How it usually goes

The kinds of work we see most.

Every piece is different, and every quote is written individually. These are the three situations restoration jobs tend to fall into.

01

Faded but intact.

Colour has dulled, edges look tired, surface needs feeding — but the structure underneath is sound. Surface re-colour, edge work, deep clean and conditioning.

Typically 7–10 days
02

Worn and weakening.

The bracket most pieces fall into. Full re-dye, corner rebuilds, handle reinforcement, edge re-paint and hardware service together.

Typically 10–14 days
03

Major rebuild.

Decades-old pieces, structural failure, lining gone, exotic leathers needing scale-by-scale work. Reconstruction rather than refinishing.

Typically 3–4 weeks

None of these are fixed packages — we look at your piece, then write the work.

Common questions

About Restoration.

Q01 How long does a typical restoration take?

Most restorations finish in 10–14 days from the moment we receive your piece. Heavier structural work — full re-dyes, lining replacement, multiple corner rebuilds — can run up to four weeks. You see photo updates at every stage.

Q02 Will it look brand new afterwards?

No, and that’s deliberate. Restoration brings leather back to a wearable, dignified state — not a factory-fresh imitation. We preserve patina and grain where we can, only intervening where the leather has failed.

Q03 Do you work on exotic leathers and suede?

Yes. We restore python, ostrich, crocodile, suede and nubuck alongside calf and aniline. Each material gets a different protocol — we’ll confirm what’s possible after seeing photos.

Q04 What if I’m outside your pickup cities?

We accept pieces by insured post from anywhere in India. Send photos on WhatsApp first — we’ll give you an estimate and packing instructions before you ship.

Q05 Is the work covered by a warranty?

All workmanship is covered for six months from the date of return. Material wear from normal use is not included, but anything that fails because of how we did the work — re-glue, re-stitch, re-paint — we fix at no cost.

Q06 How is the price determined?

Each piece is quoted individually after we see photos. The tier (light, standard, major) and material drive the estimate. You see the full written quote before any work begins — no commitment until you approve.
