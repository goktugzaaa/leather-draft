Common questions

Before you send your piece in.

How long does a restoration take?

Most restorations finish in 10–14 days. Heavier structural work — re-soling boots, re-upholstering a sofa, full re-dye on a jacket — can take up to four weeks. We share a clear timeline with your free estimate.

Do you service non-luxury brands?

Yes. We restore everyday leather just as carefully as luxury pieces — the craft is the same. Whether it’s a heirloom Hermès or a well-loved everyday tote, send us a photo and we’ll tell you what we can do.

Is doorstep pickup really free?

Free pickup and return across our 12 pickup cities, fully insured. Outside our pickup network we accept pieces by post from anywhere in India — also insured both ways.

Which cities do you cover?

Doorstep pickup runs in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Chandigarh, Gurgaon, Noida and Surat. Elsewhere in India, we work by insured post.

How much will it cost?

Every piece is different, so every estimate is hand-priced. Send us a few photos on WhatsApp or through the form below and we’ll reply with a clear quote within four hours. No card, no commitment.

Is the work guaranteed?

Every restoration carries a six-month workmanship warranty. If the work doesn’t hold, we redo it. Your piece is returned with protective wrapping and a small bottle of leather conditioner.
